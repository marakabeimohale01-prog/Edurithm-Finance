Who Can Apply

Eligibility Requirements

We focus exclusively on students at Lesotho's institutions of higher learning. Here's what you need to qualify.

Enrolled at a local institution

You must be a currently enrolled student at a college or university in Lesotho.

Age 18 – 25 years

Applicants must be between 18 and 25 years old at the time of application.

Active sponsorship / funding

You must have a reliable source of sponsorship from any recognized sponsor provider.

Valid student ID

A current student identification card from your institution is required at application.
